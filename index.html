Regal Period

753 BC - 509 BC

Rome was founded in 753 BC by Romulus and Remus. It was ruled by seven kings during this period.

Republican Period

509 BC - 27 BC

Rome transitioned from a monarchy to a republic during this period. Notable events include the Punic Wars and the rise of Julius Caesar.

Imperial Period

27 BC - 476 AD

Octavian, later known as Augustus, became the first Roman Emperor in 27 BC, marking the beginning of the Imperial period. It ended with the fall of the Western Roman Empire in 476 AD.
